🐒 Open in TelegramWhat is this?

Crypto culture already has its own language. Its own humor. Its own pain. It turned cartoon dogs into billion-dollar assets. It got millions of people emotionally attached to JPEGs. It created entire economies out of inside jokes.

But for all the memes, the threads, the screenshots sent at 3am to group chats nobody will remember by morning, there's never been music that actually belongs to the culture. Nothing that captures what it feels like to watch your portfolio melt, or what that first real win sounds like, or the strange calm that comes after you've held through something most people would have sold.

APESONUS is that music.

Seven fictional artist characters, each one pulled directly from the trenches. You've met these people in every group chat, every cycle, every market crash. The eternal bull. The poet who turns liquidation into a love song. The shill who knows exactly what he is. The duo who says what everyone's thinking at 3am without filtering any of it.

The stories never run out because the market keeps writing new ones every day. Every pump, every rug, every sideways week is new source material. The content is infinite and it's happening in real time.

But APESONUS isn't just something you listen to. It's a place you show up to. Check in on the market mood. Compete in challenges. Predict what's next. Back the artist you believe in. Earn $ONUS for being early and staying consistent. The music gets you in. The culture keeps you.

What's live today is just the start. Companions, governance, collectibles, the token — each piece rolls out as the community grows. Carefully. Earned, not rushed.

How Rewards Work

Everything in APESONUS is designed so that participating and earning happen at the same time. The more you show up, the more you accumulate.

$ONUS — The Culture Currency

$ONUS is how the culture keeps score. You don't buy it. You earn it through every action in the app — streaks, check-ins, challenges, and backing your artist.

A Genesis holder earns 12× more $ONUS than a free user for the same action. That gap compounds with every check-in, every streak, every challenge.

Daily Earnings

Market Pulse — Check in once per day (Bullish, Bearish, or Neutral). Earn $ONUS at your tier multiplier. Takes one tap.

7-Day Streaks — Listen and check in every day for 7 consecutive days. When the streak completes, you earn a bonus at your tier multiplier. Miss a day and it resets. Genesis cardholders get a one-day shield that protects the streak once per cycle.

Challenge Arena

Each week the Challenge Arena focuses on one artist. The challenges that week revolve around their music — recognize a snippet, name the track, match the mood. You need to actually listen to that artist's catalog to get the answers right. Correct answers earn $ONUS and Telegram Stars.

Weekly Chart

Track which songs and artists are running the culture each week. The weekly chart ranks tracks by unique listeners, showing who's on top and who's climbing.

Artist Backing

Verified subscribers can pick one artist to back. Every week, you earn $ONUS based on how many unique listeners your backed artist gets. Each unique listen earns you a small amount, multiplied by your verification tier.

On top of your tier, there's a loyalty multiplier that grows the longer you stay with the same artist:

1×
Weeks 1–4
1.5×
Weeks 5–8
2×
Week 9+

This stacks with your tier. A Genesis holder (3×) who has backed the same artist for 9+ weeks earns at 3× × 2× = 6× the base rate.

You can switch your backed artist once every 30 days. If you switch, your loyalty timer resets to week 1. You keep everything you've already earned, but your future rate drops back to 1× loyalty. Chase whoever's hot this week at 1×, or ride with your artist at 2× because you've been there from the start.

Mystery Drops

New tracks drop completely blind. No artist name, no title, no cover art — everything is blurred. All you get is the audio. Listen, figure out who made it from the sound alone, and guess. Correct guesses earn $ONUS and Stars. Once the reveal window closes, the track is unblurred and moves into the artist's catalog.

Governance — $ONUS holders shape the platform. Vote on directions, propose features, decide how the culture evolves.

Collectibles — Limited drops tied to milestones. Your Genesis Card is the first collectible in a much larger story.

$ONUS Token — When the community is proven and the culture carries weight, $ONUS graduates from in-platform currency to something bigger. The 10B supply is already capped. The distribution is already underway.
