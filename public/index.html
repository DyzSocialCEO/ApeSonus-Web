The Story

Crypto has quietly built a civilization of its own, with a private language, a humor that only lands after midnight, and a peculiar intimacy with loss. It made a fortune out of a cartoon dog and turned inside jokes into industries, and none of this needs explaining to the people who lived it.

And yet with all of it, the memes, the threads, the conversations that fill group chats at three in the morning, there has never been music that actually belonged to any of this. Nothing that sounded like watching a portfolio dissolve in real time, and certainly nothing for that quiet, stubborn feeling that arrives after you've held through something most people were already halfway to selling.

APESONUS is that music.

Seven artists, each of them someone you already recognise, because you've sat in group chats with every one of them. The bull who refuses to stop believing, the poet who turns a liquidation into a love song, the shill who's somehow in on the joke about himself, and the quiet one who finally says the thing everybody else has been thinking for days.

The stories don't run out because the market keeps creating them. A rug or a pump is a song, and even those slow, sideways weeks where nothing moves and everyone's quietly losing it become one too. It's all material.

But APESONUS is not only something you listen to, it is somewhere you show up. You check in on the mood of the market, you build a streak by returning each day, and the music is what gets you in while the culture is what keeps you coming back.

What is live today is only the opening chapter. More is being built in the background, richer interactions with the characters, new music arriving on its own schedule, and eventually a kind of influence from the community itself on where APESONUS goes next. Each piece ships when it is genuinely ready, and until then, showing up consistently is how you stay in the room where all of this is being decided.

Built for Telegram. Feels Like Home.

How Points Work

APESONUS is free to stream for everyone. Points are a simple recognition count that accumulates as you use the app, with no race, no cap, and no expiry.

How you earn them

There is one way to earn points inside APESONUS today. Complete a 7-day listening streak, and on the seventh day 100 points land in your balance. Miss a day and the streak resets to one. That is the whole system.

What points do

For now, points exist as a quiet record of the weeks you showed up. They have no cash value, cannot be withdrawn, cannot be traded for real money, and are not an investment of any kind.

More ways to use points may arrive as the platform matures. Anything we build will be announced before it ships.
