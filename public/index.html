The Story

Crypto has quietly built a civilization of its own, with a private language, a humor that only lands after midnight, and a peculiar intimacy with loss. It made a fortune out of a cartoon dog and turned inside jokes into industries, and none of this needs explaining to the people who lived it.

And yet with all of it, the memes, the threads, the conversations that fill group chats at three in the morning, there has never been music that actually belonged to any of this. Nothing that sounded like watching a portfolio dissolve in real time, and certainly nothing for that quiet, stubborn feeling that arrives after you've held through something most people were already halfway to selling.

APESONUS is that music.

Seven artists, each of them someone you already recognise, because you've sat in group chats with every one of them. The bull who refuses to stop believing, the poet who turns a liquidation into a love song, the shill who's somehow in on the joke about himself, and the quiet one who finally says the thing everybody else has been thinking for days.

The stories don't run out because the market keeps creating them. A rug or a pump is a song, and even those slow, sideways weeks where nothing moves and everyone's quietly losing it become one too. It's all material.

What is here today is the opening chapter. More tracks are arriving on their own schedule, and the direction of where APESONUS goes next is something the culture itself decides. Show up consistently, and you are part of the room where all of this is being shaped.
