The Story

Crypto has quietly built a civilization of its own, with a private language, a humor that only lands after midnight, and a peculiar intimacy with loss. It made a fortune out of a cartoon dog and turned inside jokes into industries, and none of this needs explaining to the people who lived it.

And yet with all of it, the memes, the threads, the conversations that fill group chats at three in the morning, there has never been music that actually belonged to any of this. Nothing that sounded like watching a portfolio dissolve in real time, and certainly nothing for that quiet, stubborn feeling that arrives after you've held through something most people were already halfway to selling.

APESONUS is that music.

Seven AI personas, each of them someone you already recognise, because you've sat in group chats with every one of them. The bull who refuses to stop believing, the poet who turns a liquidation into a love song, the shill who's somehow in on the joke about himself, and the quiet one who finally says the thing everybody else has been thinking for days.

The stories don't run out because the market keeps creating them. A rug or a pump is a song, and even those slow, sideways weeks where nothing moves and everyone's quietly losing it become one too. It's all material.

But APESONUS is not only something you listen to, it is somewhere you show up, checking in on the mood of the market, competing in the weekly arena, making your call on what comes next, and backing the artist whose story you actually believe in. $ONUS is the quiet record of you having been there, and the music is what gets you in, while the culture is what keeps you coming back.

What is live today is only the opening chapter, and more is being built in the background, deeper arenas, richer interactions with the characters, and eventually a kind of influence from the community itself on where APESONUS goes next. Each piece ships when it is genuinely ready, and until then, showing up consistently is how you stay in the room where all of this is being decided.

How Rewards Work

Everything in APESONUS is built on a simple idea, which is that showing up and being rewarded should happen at the same moment, without checklists, without manufactured daily chores, and without the kind of engagement loops that feel like a second job. The app pays attention to what you were already going to do if you liked it here.

$ONUS, The Culture Currency

$ONUS is how the culture keeps score of itself. You cannot buy it and you cannot sell it, and it accumulates in the background as you move through the app, whether you are checking in, extending a streak, voting on a forecast, competing in the arena, or backing the artist whose story you believe in. Think of it less as a balance and more as a receipt for having been present while something was being built.

$ONUS is an in-app culture currency with no cash value. It cannot be withdrawn, traded for real money, or sent outside APESONUS. It is not a security, not an investment, and APESONUS makes no representations or promises about any future form $ONUS may take.

The Founders Pass

The Founders Pass is five hundred Telegram Stars, paid once, and after that the transaction is finished. There are no renewals, no subscriptions, no escalating tiers to manage, and what it unlocks stays unlocked for as long as the app exists. You collect $ONUS at twice the base rate, permanently, and if you go quiet for a stretch, you come back to the same rate you left on. Free users collect at one quarter of the base, and there is no middle option because the Pass is the one decision worth making.

Daily Rhythms

The auto-daily bonus for WAGMI members requires nothing more than opening the app, at which point a hundred $ONUS appears in your balance, resetting every twenty-four hours per account with no check-in, no confirmation, and no friction.

The Market Pulse is a single daily tap where you commit to a reading on the mood of the market, whether bullish, bearish, or neutral, and you collect at your current rate while also getting to see which way the broader culture is leaning before anyone else commits.

The seven-day streak is exactly what it sounds like, which is to say you listen and check in for seven consecutive days, and when the streak closes, you receive a bonus at whatever rate you are currently on. Miss a day and the counter resets. WAGMI members carry a one-day shield that quietly saves the streak once per cycle, for the days when life does what life does.

The Challenge Arena

Each week the arena focuses on a single artist, and every challenge that week is built around their catalogue, whether that means recognising a snippet, naming a track, or matching a mood to a song. You have to have actually listened to know the answers, which is the point. Correct answers pay out in $ONUS and, occasionally, Telegram Stars.

The Weekly Chart

The weekly chart ranks tracks by unique listeners rather than total plays, which means it reflects how many different people a song actually reached, not how many times one devoted fan put it on repeat. It is the cleanest signal of what the room is actually listening to this week.

Artist Backing

WAGMI members can back a single artist at a time, and each week you collect $ONUS based on the unique listeners that artist pulls across the platform, with every listen earning you a small amount multiplied by your current rate.

Alongside that base, there is a loyalty multiplier that grows the longer you stay with the same artist:

It stacks with everything else. A WAGMI member at two times base, who has backed the same artist for nine weeks or more, is collecting at two times two, which is four times the base rate, and with a MEGA SURGE running on top of that, at twelve.

You can switch your backed artist once every thirty days, and if you do, the loyalty timer resets to week one, though you keep everything you have already collected. The choice is essentially between chasing whoever is hot this week at 1× or staying with the artist you believed in from the start at 2×, and one of those decisions compounds significantly over time.
