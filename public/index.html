The story

Crypto quietly built a civilization of its own. It has a private language and a humor that only lands after midnight, it made a fortune out of a cartoon dog, and nobody who lived through it needs any of this explained.

And for all of it, there has never been music that actually belonged to it — nothing that sounded like watching a portfolio dissolve in real time, or like the stubborn feeling of holding through what everyone else was already halfway to selling.

APESONUS is that music.

A growing cast of characters, each one familiar because you've sat in group chats with all of them. There's a bull who refuses to stop believing and a poet who turns a liquidation into a love song. The rest you'll recognise on sight.

The stories don't run out, because the market keeps writing them. A rug is a song. So is the slow sideways week where nothing moves and everyone is quietly losing it.

Call & Back

Play the music. Read the market.

Two contests of skill, built into the app. Both run on Spins.

Call

Conviction

A fresh board of new coins every day. Call the one you think runs — first to call it claims it, and it leaves the board. If it survives the gauntlet, your entry comes back multiplied. If it doesn't, the Spins are spent.

One find, one scout  ·  earn Spins
Back

Back an archetype

Every week, back the character you think pulls the most streams — the numbers stay hidden until it's over. Everyone's stake goes into one pool and the winners split all of it. The house takes nothing out.

Read the week  ·  earn Spins
Spins · Embers · Rewards

The more you play, the more you earn.

A rewards layer runs under the music. Show up, and it pays you back.

Spins

The fuel. Spins play the music and enter Call and Back. Pick some up, or earn more by reading the market right.

Embers

Your standing. Every track you play earns an Ember, and they never reset. The more you've listened, the more you hold.

Rewards

Partners bring sponsored rewards to the community, shared out by Embers. Listen more, and a bigger cut is yours when a drop lands.

Spins are a play credit with no cash value. Call and Back are skill-based, not gambling, and no purchase is necessary to earn Embers or receive rewards. You need to be 18 or older to take part.  See how it all works →
