The Story

Crypto has quietly built a civilization of its own, with a private language, a humor that only lands after midnight, and a peculiar intimacy with loss. It made a fortune out of a cartoon dog and turned inside jokes into industries, and none of this needs explaining to the people who lived it.

And yet with all of it, the memes, the threads, the conversations that fill group chats at three in the morning, there has never been music that actually belonged to any of this. Nothing that sounded like watching a portfolio dissolve in real time, and certainly nothing for that quiet, stubborn feeling that arrives after you've held through something most people were already halfway to selling.

APESONUS is that music.

A growing cast of characters, each one familiar because you've sat in group chats with all of them. The bull who refuses to stop believing. The poet who turns a liquidation into a love song. The shill who's somehow in on the joke about himself. The quiet one who finally says the thing everybody else has been thinking for days.

The stories don't run out because the market keeps creating them. A rug or a pump is a song. The slow, sideways weeks where nothing moves and everyone's quietly losing it become one too. It's all material.

What's here is the opening chapter. More tracks arrive on their own schedule, more characters join when the work calls for them, and the direction this goes is shaped by the people who show up early and keep showing up.

The Floor

Listen. Back Artists. Share the Drop.

Under the music is a rewards layer. The more you back the artists you believe in, the larger your share when a reward pool drops.

// 01
Listen
Five free plays a day, on any track. After that, a play costs one Ammo, and one Ammo is one cent.
// 02
Back an artist
Playing an artist builds your position in them. Backing one early, while they are still quiet, grows your position the most. Stay active and it holds; drift away and it fades to whoever stayed.
// 03
The drop
As the community plays, a pool builds, put up by us and our partners. When the moment is right it drops, split across everyone holding a position by how much they back and how long they have shown up.
// 04
Cash out
Your share lands in your balance. Cash it out in USDC on the Solana network, to your own wallet, whenever you choose.

The pools come from us and our partners, not from other people's money. A position is a record of your activity, it is not an investment, and you need to be 18 or older to take part.
