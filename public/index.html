The Call

Every day the room tries to call tomorrow's five most played tracks in exact order. The pot is a slice of every dollar that came in that day, so the more the room spends, the more there is to take. Nobody calls it clean and it rolls into tomorrow, and the number on the wall keeps climbing.

Entry is about two dollars, the going rate for being sure of yourself. The play counts stay sealed until the day settles, so there is nothing to copy and nobody to follow. The only edge is sitting in the room, listening, and doing the reading. You don't have to nail all five to eat, get four, or three, or just the top track, and you still land a rung. Everyone on the same rung splits it even.

And every Spin you play is a ticket in The Draw. Five names pulled every night, weighted by how much you played, paid in real money, first name out takes the biggest pile. The pick is seeded from the chain after the day closes, so anyone with a calculator and trust issues can re-run it and get the same five names. We insist on being checked. It's a therapy thing.
